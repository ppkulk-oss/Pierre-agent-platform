## üöÜ TRANSPORT PROBLEMS

### Missed Train
- **Shinkansen:** Can usually take next train with same ticket (unreserved seats)
- **Reserved seats:** Go to ticket office to change (usually free)
- **Local trains:** Just take next one

### Lost in Station
- Major stations have English information desks
- Station staff usually helpful
- Use Google Maps - works underground!
- Look for "Information" or ask "Eigo wa dekimasu ka?" (Do you speak English?)

### No Seats on Shinkansen
- Unreserved cars: Walk through to find seats
- Worst case: Stand in deck area (still gets you there)
- Next time: Reserve seats in advance

### Late Night Transport
- Trains stop ~midnight
- **If stranded:**
  - Taxi (expensive but works)
  - Capsule hotel near station
  - 24-hour McDonald's/caf√© until first train (~5 AM)

## üè® HOTEL PROBLEMS

### Room Issues
- Contact front desk immediately
- Most hotels will fix or move you
- Language barrier: Use Google Translate or hotel phone translation service

### Lost Reservation
- Have confirmation number ready
- Show booking confirmation email
- If truly lost: Use booking app/Expedia to resolve
- Backup: Walk to nearby hotel

### Early Arrival / Late Departure
- **Early:** Ask to store luggage, explore area
- **Late:** Most hotels store luggage for free
- **Day rooms:** Some hotels offer for fee
- **Alternatives:** Coin lockers at stations

## üí≥ MONEY EMERGENCIES

### Lost/Stolen Credit Card
1. **Call immediately to cancel:** Number on back of card or app
2. **Report to police** (for insurance claim)
3. **Use backup card** (you brought multiple, right?)
4. **Emergency cash:** Western Union, embassy assistance

### ATM Won't Dispense Cash
- **Try different card** (Visa vs Mastercard vs debit)
- **Try different ATM brand:** 7-Eleven, Japan Post, Citibank
- **Try different amount:** Some have per-transaction limits
- **Bank transfer:** Use app to send to trusted contact in Japan

### Ran Out of Cash
- **ATMs at every 7-Eleven** (seriously, everywhere)
- **Credit cards** accepted at most major places
- **IC card** can be used at many convenience stores
- **Currency exchange** at airports, major stations

## üé¢ UNIVERSAL STUDIES DISASTER SCENARIOS

### Tickets Sold Out
- **Check Klook, Voyagin** - sometimes have inventory
- **Single rider tickets** at gate (limited)
- **Go anyway:** Sometimes day-of tickets available
- **Backup day:** Flexible with Osaka schedule?

### Express Pass Doesn't Work
- **Go to Guest Services** immediately
- **Keep receipt/proof of purchase**
- **Usually resolved quickly**
- **Single rider lines** as fallback

### Too Crowded to Enjoy
- **Arrive at rope drop** (before opening)
- **Hit headliners first** (Nintendo World, Harry Potter)
- **Use single rider** for shorter waits
- **Shows/shops** during peak ride times (11 AM - 4 PM)

### Child Scared/Won't Ride
- **Child swap** program available (ask cast member)
- **Alternatives:** Shows, character meets, parades
- **No shame:** Not every kid loves coasters

## üóæ ITINERARY FLEXIBILITY

### If You Need to Skip Something
**Can Skip:**
- Any single temple (they're all beautiful)
- Shopping districts (except maybe Harajuku for teens)
- Secondary observation deck (pick Skytree OR Shibuya Sky)

**Don't Skip:**
- Fushimi Inari (Kyoto highlight)
- USJ (kids will remember this forever)
- Ryokan experience (unique cultural immersion)

### If You Want to Add Something
**Easy Additions:**
- Extra temple/shrine (they're everywhere)
- Shopping district (Harajuku, Shinsaibashi)
- Food tour/market visit
- Extra onsen time (Hakone)

### If Running Behind Schedule
- **Shorten temple visits** (walk through vs full exploration)
- **Skip shopping** (can do at airport)
- **Take taxi** instead of transit (saves time, costs more)
- **Group meals** (eat while transiting)

## ‚úàÔ∏è FLIGHT EMERGENCIES

### Delayed/Canceled Flight
- **United app** - check status first
- **Rebooking:** Use app, call, or airport desk
- **Compensation:** Depends on reason, airline policy
- **Accommodation:** Airline may provide if overnight delay

### Missed Connection
- **United will rebook** on next available
- **If next day:** Ask about hotel/meal vouchers
- **Stay calm:** Happens often, airlines handle it

### Lost Luggage
- **Report immediately** at baggage claim office
- **Get reference number**
- **Most arrive within 24 hours**
- **Buy essentials** (airline reimburses reasonable amounts)
