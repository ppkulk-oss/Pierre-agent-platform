## ğŸ¥— VEGETARIAN SURVIVAL GUIDE (For Riya)

### Safe Foods:
- **Rice:** Plain, onigiri (check fillings), sushi rice
- **Noodles:** Soba, udon (check broth - dashi may have fish)
- **Tempura:** Vegetable tempura (common)
- **Tofu:** Agedashi tofu, yudofu
- **Sushi:** Tamago (egg), kappa (cucumber), avocado rolls
- **Sides:** Edamame, pickles, miso soup (check - often fish-based)

### Things to Avoid:
- Dashi (broth) - usually fish-based
- Miso soup (often contains fish stock)
- Many "vegetable" dishes have fish flakes (bonito)

### Magic Words:
- **"Watashi wa bejitarian desu"** (I am vegetarian)
- **"Sakana to niku tabemasen"** (I don't eat fish or meat)
- **Point to items and ask:** "Kore wa bejitarian desu ka?" (Is this vegetarian?)
