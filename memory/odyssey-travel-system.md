## ğŸ¯ FAMILY TRAVEL PRINCIPLES

### Balance
- Mix of adult interests (culture, food) + kid interests (fun, active)
- Schedule downtime (afternoon hotel breaks)
- One "big" activity per day max

### Flexibility
- Build in buffer time
- Have backup indoor activities
- Don't over-schedule
- Be willing to skip things

### Food Strategy
- Research dietary accommodations ahead
- Identify 2-3 "safe" restaurants per area
- Always have convenience store backup
- Don't force adventurous eating every meal

### Budget Consciousness
- Track estimated costs in planning phase
- Identify splurge-worthy experiences
- Find areas to save (lunch vs dinner, transport)
- Account for hidden costs (tips, resort fees, transport)
